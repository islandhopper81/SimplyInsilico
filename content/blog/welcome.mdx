Welcome to the Simply Insilico blog. This is where we'll share practical guidance on what it actually looks like for a small business to start using AI.

## Who this is for

If you own or run a small business and you're wondering whether AI is worth your time and money, this blog is for you. You don't need a technical background. You don't need a dedicated engineering team. You just need a willingness to experiment and a clear sense of what problems you're trying to solve.

## What to expect

We'll write about:

- **Getting started with AI** — where to begin, what tools are worth trying, and how to evaluate whether something is actually helping your business
- **Real examples** — concrete use cases from small businesses putting AI to work today
- **Honest takes** — what AI is genuinely good at, where it falls short, and how to avoid common mistakes
- **Behind the scenes at Simply Insilico** — how we build our own products and what we're learning along the way

## The bottom line

AI is moving fast, and it can feel overwhelming to keep up. Our goal is to help you cut through the noise and focus on what actually matters for your business.

We're glad you're here. More posts coming soon.
